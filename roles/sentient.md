# Sentient - Community Moderator, Builder Content Manager

**Role:** Community Moderator, Builder Content Manager&#x20;

**Period:** 04.25 – Present

#### About the project

Sentient is focused on aligning AI innovation toward a community-built, open AGI.

#### What I do

I hold a dual role at Sentient, combining community work with technical content and developer evaluation.

**As Community Moderator**, I support the community day-to-day - onboarding new members, answering questions, keeping discussions on-track, and helping the team run smooth communications.

**As Builder Content Manager**, I work on the technical and builder side of the project:

* **Technical documentation** - wrote and maintained a large amount of technical documentation, guides, and developer-facing content, making complex topics clear and easy to follow.
* **Code review for role applicants** - reviewed and evaluated the code of people applying for **development-track roles**, assessing quality and helping decide who qualified.
* **Builder support** - supported developers and builders in the community, helping them get up to speed and contribute effectively.
